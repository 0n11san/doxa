About Us

Doxa Health and Wellness, LLC

Doxa Health and Wellness LLC provides comprehensive behavioral health services via Telehealth solutions.

We are a private practice made up of a team of Psychiatric Nurse Practitioners committed to providing the highest quality mental health services to the Maryland area.

Telehealth services provide a rapid response to your treatment. We provide medication management for a variety of mental health issues, including ADHD/ADD, anxiety, depression, schizophrenia, mood disorders, and more. We diagnose and treat children, adolescents, adults, and the geriatric population.

Working with Doxa means working to find the right fit with one of our prescribers, all of whom have been experienced registered nurses for many years and are now licensed Psychiatric Nurse Practitioners dedicated to restoring your mental well-being. Our prescribers bring with them a wealth of knowledge and experience. Each are committed to working with you in the way that works best for you.

Andin Namanga, CRNP-PMH

Andinwe (Andin) Namanga, PMHNP, is a board-certified psychiatric mental health nurse practitioner with over 10 years experience in health care. She is licensed to provide psychiatric evaluation and treatment in Maryland. During the past decade, she has been caring for patients with a wide variety of mental health conditions. Andin’s experience includes caring for patients in outpatient settings and inpatient acute psychiatric units.

Andin graduated from Walden University Magna cum laude in 2020. She is experienced in providing psychiatric care to children, adolescents and adults. She treats a variety of psychiatric conditions including anxiety, depression, ADHD, schizophrenia and bipolar disorder.

Andine describes herself as a person who goes above and beyond to address her patient’s needs. She cares for her patient’s overall well-being and is always willing to collaborate with the patient and other providers to deliver the safest and most effective treatment. Andin has been an active member of the American Psychiatric Nurses Association since 2013. She is a strong advocate for the destigmatization of mental illness.

On a personal side, Andin is married and is a mom of three beautiful children. She believes her faith in God has helped her remain grounded and has played a big part in who she is.

Andin strives to provide patients with the best experience using a genuine caring approach. You will find that Andin is patient, understanding and very knowledgeable.

Mission 

To enhance the quality of life of individuals dealing with mental health challenges by providing consistently high quality psychiatric care services.

Vision

Meeting the needs of vulnerable people suffering from mental health problems, within Maryland and nationwide.
