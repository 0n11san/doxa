Services

Psych Evaluation and Medication Management
At Doxa Health and Wellness LLC, we provide comprehensive psychiatric evaluation and medication management. We collaborate with you to determine which treatment option will help improve your symptoms. We understand that each person is unique and our clinicians create a customized treatment plan for each individual. We provide supportive therapy and patient education on factor that can influence your mental health such as stress, nutrition and physical activity. Our aim is to help you feel better, faster and in the safest way possible.

Conditions treated include:
Generalized Anxiety Disorder, Major Depressive Disorder, Attention-Deficit / Hyperactivity Disorder, Bipolar Disorder, Schizophrenia, Panic Disorder, Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder and other mood/anxiety or behavioral problems.


* Sole therapy services are currently unavailable at Doxa Health and Wellness LLC. When it is determined that therapy is appropriate, a referral will be provided.
