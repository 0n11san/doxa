Get Started

Contact Us
Call our office number, provide us with your full name and email address. A link will be sent for you to complete the intake forms. Familiarize yourself with our practice policies and procedures.

Forms
When you reach out to our office for the first time as a new patient, we’ll ask you to complete some initial forms which will be sent to you electronically using our digital patient check-in platform, ICANotes.

Please complete relevant forms before our first meeting. We ask that you give special consideration to the Consent For psychiatric Services/Practice Policies form, which contains important information regarding your privacy, the psychiatric services offered, as well as insurance and billing information. Should you have additional questions about our procedures, matters of confidentiality, or insurance and billing, please ask us to clarify. We expect it will take 30 minutes to complete the forms.

All required registration and practice forms have to be completed prior to being seen. If we do not have your completed forms on file prior to your visits we may ask you to reschedule your appointment.

ID and Insurance Card (if applicable)
Remember to upload a picture of your insurance card and driver’s license (for minors, upload the parent's drivers license).

Payment
Ensure you have an active credit card of your choice, including your Flexible Spending Account (FSA) or Health Savings Account (HSA) card on file. After your insurance has paid their portion, we will notify you of your remaining balance (if applicable) and charge your card on file. Receipts will be available via ICANotes. Your credit card information will always be fully protected by our off-site, card-processing partner Stripe.
