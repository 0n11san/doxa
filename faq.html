Frequently Asked Questions

Doxa Health and Wellness, LLC

Are you taking new patients?
Yes, Doxa Health and Wellness LLC is open to take new patients via Telehealth platform ONLY at this time until further notice.

Do you treat substance abuse?
No, we currently do not provide services for substance abuse, however we do provide referrals to providers and facilities that do.

What is a Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner-BC (PMHNP-BC)?
PMHNP-BCs are licensed and board certified with the Board of Nursing within their respective state. They are advanced practice nurses who hold Masters, Doctorate and/ or Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) degrees. PMHNPs are experienced and trained professionals who provide holistic care, aimed at treating the WHOLE person across their lifespan (children to geriatric). They provide comprehensive evaluation and assessments; they diagnose, order, perform and interpret testing. They also prescribe medications and provide psychotherapy. NPs also provide patient education on lifestyle changes and encourage self-care to promote whole person wellness.

Do you provide therapy?
Our services include supportive therapy, cognitive behavioral therapy and solution-focused therapy alongside medication management visits. Therapy sessions alone are currently not offered at our facility as of yet. However, we do provide referrals to therapists, social workers and counselors as well as collaborate with them.

Do you take my insurance?
We are in network with Aetna, Cigna, and Medicare. We are out of network with all other insurance providers at this time but are working towards becoming in network with them soon.
