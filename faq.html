Frequently Asked Questions

Doxa Health and Wellness LLC

Are you taking new patients?
Yes, Doxa Health and Wellness LLC is open to take new patients via our Telehealth platform ONLY. To request an appointment, Please call the office to schedule.

Do you treat substance abuse?
No, we currently do not provide services for substance abuse, however we do provide referrals to providers and facilities that do.

What is a Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner-BC (PMHNP-BC)?
PMHNP-BC are certified with the Board of Nursing and licensed within their respective state(s) to provide psychiatric services. They are advanced practice nurses who hold Masters, Doctorate and/ or Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) degrees. PMHNPs are experienced and trained professionals who provide holistic care, aimed at treating the WHOLE person across their lifespan (children to geriatric). They provide comprehensive evaluations and assessments; diagnose, order, perform and interpret testing. PMHNPs also prescribe medications and provide psychotherapy. PMHNPs also provide patient education on lifestyle changes and encourage self-care to promote whole person wellness. Psychiatric Nurse Practitioners can see clients with commercial insurance, Medicare, Medicaid and private pay.

Do you provide therapy?
Our services include supportive therapy, cognitive behavioral therapy and solution-focused therapy alongside medication management visits. Therapy sessions alone are currently not offered at our facility as of yet. However, we do provide referrals to therapists, social workers and counselors as well as collaborate with them.

What insurance do you accept? How can I learn more about what my insurance covers and what my out of pocket cost will be?
We are currently in-network with several insurers. Doxa is working towards becoming in network with more insurers as time goes on. Please continue to check back for updates.
Coverage varies depending on your insurance plan and the services you need. You can call your insurance company to find out what psychiatric services are covered under your insurance.

What types of services do you offer and how do I determine which ones I need?
If you have been struggling with challenges related to your mental health and need to talk to somebody, we can assist you in scheduling an appointment with one of our clinicians. Doxa Health and Wellness LLC provides comprehensive psychiatric evaluations and medication management.

What age groups do you work with?
We work with clients, from children age 6 to adults of any age.

What do I need to participate in Telehealth?
You will need a device that allows both audio and video communication. This may be a computer, tablet, phone, etc. Doxa sends you a link to your device which you simply click on and are connected to a video call. With telehealth, you do not have to come in to an office.
Our clinicians provide face-face telehealth through a secure, HIPAA-compliant video conferencing platform which is easy and simple to connect to.

What if I would rather come to the office for appointments?
Unfortunately, at this time, Doxa Health and Wellness LLC only conducts Telehealth sessions.

Can you provide service to me out of state?
Our clinicians are currently only Licensed to practice within Maryland.

Can you fill out a form I need for school/ work/ court/ disability accommodations/ etc?
We'll gladly assist in completing any requested forms. In order to accurately complete forms it is a required that care has been established for at least 6 months, especially for completion of disability forms. More details are provided in our psychiatric Services/Practice Policies form.
